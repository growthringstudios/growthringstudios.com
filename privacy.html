Privacy Policy

Effective June 20, 2026 · Growth Ring Studios LLC

The short version: Calculogger collects no personal data, requires no account, and works entirely on your device. Nothing you enter is ever sent to us or any third party.

What we collect

Nothing. Calculogger performs all calculations locally on your device. No measurements, inputs, results, or usage patterns are transmitted, stored on our servers, or shared with anyone.

Accounts & login

Calculogger requires no account, registration, or login of any kind.

Analytics & tracking

We do not use analytics tools, advertising identifiers, crash reporters, or any third-party tracking SDKs. There are no cookies or local storage entries written beyond what the app needs to function offline (the Progressive Web App service worker cache).

Network requests

Once installed, Calculogger operates fully offline. The app does not make any network requests during normal use. The initial load fetches app assets from our hosting provider; no user data is included in those requests.

Third-party services

Calculogger does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Children

Calculogger is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal data, no special handling is required.

Changes to this policy

If we ever add features that collect data, we will update this policy before those features ship and note the new effective date at the top of this page.
