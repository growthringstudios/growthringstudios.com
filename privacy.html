Privacy Policy

Effective June 20, 2026 · Growth Ring Studios LLC

Calculogger is a calculator app for sawyers, arborists, and urban wood programs. This policy explains what data we collect — and what we don't.

We do not collect any personal information.

Calculogger performs all calculations locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any data you enter into the app. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party data sharing.

What stays on your device

All inputs you enter — log dimensions, species, prices, moisture content, or any other values — exist only on your device while the app is open. Nothing is sent to any server.

Cookies and tracking

Calculogger does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools.

Children's privacy

Calculogger does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above. Given that we collect no data, we do not anticipate meaningful changes.
